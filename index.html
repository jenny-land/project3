Full Story

I'm a UX designer and researcher focused on interaction systems and research-driven clarity. I'm drawn to complex human problems — where workflows, constraints, and behavior intersect — and I work to make them feel intuitive and grounded.

Nine years in early childhood education sharpened how I observe, listen, and translate what people can't always articulate into systems that actually work for them.
